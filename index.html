About

Welcome to my homepage! My name is Zhen Zhu, a second-year master student in the VLR Group of Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I am advised by Professor Xiang Bai. I received my Bachelor's degree from school of Computer Science and Technology, HUST in 2017. My research interests lie in generative adversarial models, semantic segmentation and object detection, etc.
